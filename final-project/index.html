Products

Super Nintendo Entertainment System

A classic home console released in 1991. Play your favourite 16-bit games by yourself or with a friend. If you’re feeling adventurous try playing some games that make use of the Super FX chip to see some impressive 16-bit 3D visuals.

Nintendo GameCube

Our second foray into 3D consoles, the Nintendo GameCube was released in 2001. Play exciting, action packed games such as Metroid Prime, or invite some friends over and play Super Smash Bros. Melee. If that’s not your style, sit back and relax as you play some Animal Crossing.

Nintendo Switch

The Nintendo Switch is our latest console release, and because of that it holds our most recent game releases. Not only can you hook the Nintendo Switch up to the TV, you can pick it up out of its stand and take it on the go in handheld mode.
