Our History

Our company was not always centered around the gaming industry, however we have always been associated with fun and enjoyment.

We started out in 1889 as a card making company; playing cards, that is.

Eventually we broke into the toy industry in 1966 creating toys such as the Love Tester.

We did not break into the gaming industry until 1972 where we created arcade games and began our line of Game & Watch handheld LCD games.

It wasn't until 1985 that we entered the home console industry with the Nintendo Entertainment System and we have been producing home consoles and games for them ever since.

Our Current Business

In this modern era, we continue to create. We have kept our business centered around our unique games and consoles and continue to try to innovate and expand with each new release, be it console or game. We hope to continue to bring enjoyment to gamers of all ages into the future!
